Daniel Lee left Bottega Veneta

The news came hours before the American Fashion Designers Association, where British Lee was nominated for two categories: International Women's Clothes Designer of the Year and International Men's Clothes Designer of the Year, and two weeks before the Fashion Awards in London, where Lee was named Designer of the Year.
